Frequently Asked Questions

How can I apply for a job through your platform?

To apply for a job through a platform, create an account, complete your profile, search for relevant jobs, and submit your application by uploading your resume and following any additional instructions. Track your application status through the platform’s dashboard.

What are the benefits of working with your HR company?

Working with our HR company provides access to top talent, customized recruitment strategies, and streamlined hiring processes. We offer support in talent acquisition, employee engagement, and retention, helping build a strong, adaptable team for long-term success.

How long does it typically take for companies to respond after the job fair?

Response times can vary depending on the company and the number of applicants, but typically, companies take one to two weeks to follow up after the job fair. If you haven’t heard back within that time frame, it’s a good idea to send a polite follow-up email to express your continued interest and ask about the next steps.

Is formal dress compulsory?

Yes, wearing formal dress is generally recommended for job fairs and campus placements as it presents a professional image to recruiters. Dressing in business attire shows that you are serious and prepared for the opportunity

What are the things to be done on the day before the campus placements?

The day before campus placements, update and print your resume, research the companies, practice your pitch, ensure your formal dress is ready, get a good night’s rest, plan your route, and prepare questions for recruiters. This will help you feel confident and ready
